Prospective Research Directions

These are research directions and projects I would be excited to explore if I join Centre College.

AI-Assisted Immersive STEM Education

Explores the use of AI and augmented reality to create interactive, immersive learning environments in engineering education. Potential directions include visual overlays for lab procedures, real-time guidance, intuitive visualization of complex engineering concepts, and adaptive feedback systems to enhance student engagement and understanding.

Related project-based cost$0

FERPA-Compliant Local AI Tools for Pedagogy and ABET Support

Exploration of locally deployed AI systems that support teaching and assessment while ensuring data privacy and FERPA compliance, including automated feedback on student work, structured ABET outcome mapping, rubric alignment checking, and streamlined documentation for continuous improvement. It would be valuable for this to develop into a campus-wide initiative.

Related project-based cost$0

Engineering Design, Human-AI Interaction, and Human-AI Collaboration

Investigation of how undergraduate students engage with AI tools during the engineering design process, with an emphasis on early-stage ideation, decision-making, and the development of effective and responsible human–AI collaboration practices. This work aims to better understand human–AI collaboration and to develop strategies that support learning, creativity, and responsible AI use in engineering education.

Related project-based cost$0
